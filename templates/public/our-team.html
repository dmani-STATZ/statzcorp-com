Stanley "Mark" Lyons

President / Owner
STATZ Corporation

Mark is the President and Owner of STATZ Corporation (Jan 2020 to Present)

Mark is a retired Army 1SG and Disabled Veteran with more than 20 years as a Cavalry Scout and Paratrooper with several deployments worldwide. Mark's education includes an M.B.A. in Business Administration from Cardinal Stritch University, a Bachelor of Science in Marketing from Columbia Southern University and Lean Six Sigma Green Belt from Villanova. Mark's career in management, leadership and supply chain management/development will prove invaluable to our customers, suppliers and the STATZ Corporation Team. Mark wants STATZ to be all about the Warfighters and takes STATZ's mission seriously for our great nation.
